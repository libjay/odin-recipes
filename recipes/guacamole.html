Chunk up the Jams Guacamole

This simple Guacamole recipe is great for any occasion

Ingredients

  • 6 Avocados
  • 2 Limes
  • 1/2 cup Pico De Gallo
  • 3 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 tbs coarse sea salt

Steps

  1. Scoop all avocado flesh into stainless steel mixing bowl and mash. Leave some chunks.
  2. Add pico de gallo and mix well
  3. add juice from both limes and salt to taste.

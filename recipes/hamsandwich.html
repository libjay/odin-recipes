Ham Sandwich

Plain Ham Sandwich

It's just a ham sandwich

Ingredients

  • Ham, as much or as little as you like
  • White bread, 2 slices
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Spread mayo on bread, or dont, who really cares. in the end we all die
  2. Put the Ham on there and "Tadaa!!!" you have a sandwich
  3. You can dress it with mustard or blood or whatever, its a ham sandwich

Ian's Wrecktal Fire Chili

This barn burner of a chili recipe is sure to delight and set alight a fire down below. Packed full of capsacin, it will float the boat of any chili head while capsizing all those to weak for these treacherous waters

Ingredients

  • 3 tbs Ancho Chili Powder
  • 2 Tbs Cayenne Pepper
  • 1/2 cup minced Jalapeno
  • 2 cans chili beans
  • 1 can crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 qts beef stock
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 4 Tbs Cumin Powder
  • 3 tbs Chili Powder
  • 1lb Rare Smoked Tri Tip Roast, cubed
  • 1/2 cup Roasted Habaneros

Steps

  1. Heat cast Iron pot on med high. Add 2tbs rendered beef fat.
  2. Add onion and saute, stirring occasionally until translucent.
  3. Add Tri-Tip and Jalapenos, stir gently, then let sit covered for 2 minutes
  4. deglaze pan with tomatoes and add all spices, return to boil
  5. Add beef stock and cover, reduce heat to low and let cook, stirring occasionally for 45min
  6. Add beans and Habeneros, cook 10 minutes
  7. Remove from heat, let stand 5 min and enjoy with your choice of toppings
